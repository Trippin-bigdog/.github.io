Felix Advisory Strategies

1% STOP LOSS STRATEGY

This algorithm operates with a simple strategy of buying SPY (S&P 500 ETF) when not already invested and the next entry time has arrived, and selling SPY if its price increases by 1% or decreases by 1% from the entry price.

OPEN PRICE REVERSION STRATEGY

The algorithm operates on a mean reversion strategy on SPY. If the opening price is more than 1% than the previous day's close, it sells SPY. Conversely, if the opening price is 1% lower than the previous day's close, it buys SPY.

SMALL CAP FLOATING PORTFOLIO

This monthly rebalancing strategy selects bottom 10 of 200 liquid stocks based on dollar volume and top 20 by market cap. The strategy is focused to capitalize on smaller caps' outperformance to capture greater returns.

SMA + NEAR LOW|HIGH

This algorithm initializes with a simple moving average (SMA) calculated from daily historical data. It trades SPY based on its proximity to the 52-week high/low and SMA, going long if near the high and uptrending, short if near low and downtrending, else flat.

TRADING WITH SMA INDICATOR

To combine indicators and backtest performance, this code initializes with SMA and 60-day high/low for AAPL. It triggers a long position if the price is 5% above the high and SMA is above the price, and a short position if 5% below the low and SMA is below the price.
